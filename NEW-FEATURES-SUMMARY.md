# 🚀 PE Mobile - NEW FEATURES IMPLEMENTED

## ✅ THREE MAJOR FEATURES ADDED:

### 1. ⏰ TIME ADJUSTMENT FEATURE

**What it does:**
- Allows techs to manually set their exact work hours for any job
- Perfect for when they forget to start/stop timers
- Calculates total hours automatically including break time

**How it works:**
- New **"⏰ Adjust Time"** button on every job card
- Set **Start Time** and **End Time** (e.g., 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM)
- Set **Break Duration** in minutes (defaults to 30 minutes)
- **Real-time calculation** shows total hours worked
- **Save** button logs the exact time to SimPro

**Example:**
- Start: 8:00 AM
- End: 5:00 PM  
- Break: 60 minutes
- **Result: 8.00 hours logged**

---

### 2. 📅 DATE NAVIGATION FEATURE

**What it does:**
- Browse jobs from any day (not just today)
- Go back to previous days to add missing time entries
- Navigate forward/backward with arrow buttons or date picker

**How it works:**
- **Date picker** at top of app (below user info)
- **← → arrow buttons** to go back/forward one day
- **Direct date selection** by tapping the date field
- **Jobs automatically reload** for selected date

**Use cases:**
- "I forgot to log time yesterday" → select yesterday's date
- "Need to add notes to Tuesday's job" → navigate to Tuesday
- "Check what I did last week" → browse previous dates

---

### 3. 🔔 END-OF-DAY REMINDERS

**What it does:**
- Automatically reminds techs to complete time entries
- Prevents missing time logs at end of work day
- Smart notifications that don't annoy users

**How it works:**
- **Daily check at 5:00 PM** on weekdays
- **Hourly reminders** after 4:00 PM if entries incomplete
- **Popup notification** shows number of incomplete jobs
- **"Review" button** highlights jobs needing attention
- **"Later" button** dismisses until next check

**Reminder features:**
- ✅ Only shows on work days (Monday-Friday)
- ✅ Only after 4:00 PM (end of work day)
- ✅ Can be dismissed (won't bug them repeatedly)
- ✅ Auto-dismisses after 30 seconds
- ✅ Highlights incomplete jobs with yellow border
